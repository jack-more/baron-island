## Player World Architecture

**World Structure:** A compact spherical memory world with seven zones that can be crossed in minutes, built like a playable documentary with missions, outfits, NPCs, story objects, and player interview unlocks.

**Main Quest:** The Route
The user carries memory objects across Baron's eras. Each delivery unlocks a story prompt
that Baron can later answer in his own voice or use to interview another player.

**Interaction Rules:**
- Click or tap to walk
- Smart follow camera
- World-native checklist
- Short NPC dialogue bubbles
- One story object delivery per zone
- Outfit customization with era unlocks
- Playable in the first minute

**Social Rules:**
- Small rooms around 8 to 10 users
- No open text chat in version one
- 3D emoji wheel for follow me, thanks, fire, respect, wait, hoop, story, and laugh
- Users can guide each other through movement and jumping

**Rights-Light Prototype Rules:**
- Use fictional team and school marks until official licenses are approved
- Use inspired colors and silhouettes instead of exact jerseys in the prototype
- Use original audio beds until music rights are cleared
- Mark exact childhood block details as player-confirmation pending
- Treat all first-person memories as placeholders until Baron records or approves them

**Era Role:** Childhood, family, first hoop imagination, and South LA origin point

**Evidence:** [BD4](https://lasentinel.net/socal-legends-baron-davis.html)

**Visual World:**
- Blue Los Angeles sky
- Warm concrete court
- Chain-link fence
- Bus stop
- Corner store
- Low-rise housing
- Grandmother home story node

**Zeitgeist Signals:**
- Older cousins and neighborhood runs
- Kids watching through the fence
- Local pride and survival pressure
- Early 1990s LA hoop culture

**Young Fan Feeling:**
The user should feel small inside a big basketball dream, with the city acting like both
pressure and propulsion.

**Mission:** Take the Shoes to the Court
Pick up a worn pair of hoop shoes near the home block and carry them to the chain-link
court. When delivered, the court lights up with kid voices and ball bounce.

**Story Unlock Prompt:**
Who first made you believe your game could take you beyond the block, and what did that
belief cost them?

**Outfit Unlocks:**
- South LA court tee
- Worn hoop shoes
- Tube socks
- Practice shorts

## Interview Capture Kit

Use these questions in the player interview before generating the licensed world.

- What place from this era still feels alive when you close your eyes?
- What object would instantly take you back to this chapter?
- What did young fans misunderstand about you then?
- What did teammates know about that era that fans never saw?
- What was the private pressure behind the public highlight?
- What sound, smell, outfit, or phrase belongs in this world?
- Which story would you only tell another player?
- Who else should have a world after yours?
